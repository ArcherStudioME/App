Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 21, 2025

1. Introduction

At ArcherStudio, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you access or use our website, services, or any platform we operate.

By using our services, creating an account, placing an order, or interacting with our site in any way, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully to understand your rights and how we manage your data in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and applicable Philippine laws.

If you do not agree with any part of this policy, we advise you to discontinue use of our services and platforms.

2. Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: Full name, email address, contact number, and delivery address.
  • Account Data: Login credentials (securely hashed), order history, and claimed vouchers.
  • Uploaded Files: Documents or images submitted for printing, editing, or design services.
  • Payment Information: Billing data and transaction confirmations (we do not store credit/debit card numbers).
  • Technical Information: IP address, device details, browser type, operating system, and timestamps of use.
  • Cookies and Analytics: Session behavior, preferences, and site interaction data via cookies and analytics tools.
3. Purpose of Collection

Your information is collected and processed for the following lawful and clearly defined purposes:

  • To fulfill print, design, and delivery service requests accurately and efficiently
  • To communicate service updates, system issues, or important account-related notices
  • To securely process transactions, including payments, refunds, and returns
  • To perform internal analytics aimed at improving user experience and service quality
  • To verify user identity when handling password recovery or access restoration requests
  • To meet legal, tax, and regulatory obligations in accordance with Philippine law
4. Consent and Data Retention

We collect and process your personal data only when necessary and with your informed consent, in accordance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012—particularly Sections 12 and 13, which allow certain exceptions.

Password reset or recovery links sent to your registered email are valid for 1 day only. This time limit is enforced to enhance the security of your account and data.

Do not share access links with anyone. These links are designed for one-time use, and once activated, they immediately expire to prevent unauthorized access and ensure user protection.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

However, we may disclose your data in the following specific situations:

  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, subpoena, court order, or other legal processes.
  • Service Fulfillment: When necessary to complete your order, such as sharing your name and contact number with delivery personnel or partner couriers.
  • User Protection: When needed to protect the rights, safety, or property of you, other users, or our platform.
6. Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your personal data. These measures include:

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmitted through our platform is protected using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption.
  • Secure Credentials: Passwords are hashed and salted before storage to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel with a legitimate purpose can access sensitive user information.
  • File Security: Uploaded files are securely stored, handled, and permanently deleted when no longer needed.

While we implement strong security practices, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely foolproof. We encourage users to maintain strong passwords and exercise caution when accessing their accounts.

7. User Rights

In accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you are entitled to the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Right to be Informed – You have the right to know how your personal data is collected, processed, stored, and shared.
  • Right to Object – You may decline or withdraw your consent to the processing of your data, unless it is required by law or contract.
  • Right to Access – You may request a copy of the personal information we currently hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification – You may ask us to correct any inaccurate or outdated personal data.
  • Right to Erasure or Blocking – You may request the deletion or blocking of your data when it is no longer necessary, or if processing is unlawful, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to Data Portability – You may obtain your personal data in a structured and commonly used format, and transmit it to another entity.
  • Right to Damages – You may claim compensation if you suffer damage due to inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, false, unlawfully obtained, or unauthorized use of your personal data.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint – You may file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if you believe your rights under the Data Privacy Act have been violated.
8. Use of Cookies and Analytics

We may use browser cookies and third-party tools (such as a secure PDF analysis API) to enhance and personalize your experience on our website. These tools help us:

  • Understand how users interact with our platform
  • Monitor performance and detect issues
  • Improve service usability and functionality
  • Automatically count pages in uploaded PDF files to calculate pricing accurately

Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow us to remember your preferences, login sessions, and past activity. You have full control over cookie usage and may disable them at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features of our service.

Uploaded PDF files are processed temporarily for page count and pricing estimation purposes only. Files are not stored or shared beyond the session and are securely discarded after processing.

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update, revise, or amend this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or service improvements. Any such changes will be posted on our official website with the updated effective date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed of how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of our services after any updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
